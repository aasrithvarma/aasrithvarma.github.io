About Me

I am a medical doctor from Hyderabad, India, with aspirations of becoming a pediatric cardiac surgeon. Alongside medicine, I write, design, and lead sustainable initiatives. My debut book, The Power of 77, is a journey into discipline, reflection, and the patterns that shape our lives.

Savière

Savière is my vision of sustainable luxury: a brand rooted in veganism, slow fashion, and mindful design. Its mission is to create timeless pieces from upcycled fabrics while reducing waste and encouraging conscious consumption. Savière embodies silent luxury — comfort, sustainability, and elegance without compromise.

Advocacy & Activism

  • Supporter of accessible and affordable healthcare for all.
  • Promoter of veganism and ethical living.
  • Environmental conservation and sustainable design activist.

My Book

The Power of 77 explores discipline, thought, and meaning in everyday actions. It inspires readers to find balance and growth through structured reflection.
