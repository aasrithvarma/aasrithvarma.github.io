About Me

I am a medical doctor and aspiring pediatric cardiac surgeon, passionate about accessible healthcare and affordability. I founded Savière, a sustainable, vegan luxury fashion brand rooted in slow fashion and eco-conscious living. I am also the author of The Power of 77, a book on inspiration and self-growth.

Beyond medicine and design, I am an activist advocating for feminism, gender equality, veganism, and environment conservation.
