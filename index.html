About

I am a medical doctor and aspiring pediatric cardiac surgeon, passionate about accessible healthcare and affordability, with a deep focus on equitable outcomes and compassionate care.

I founded Savière, a sustainable, vegan luxury fashion brand rooted in upcycling, slow design, and ethical production, and authored The Power of 77 on inspiration and growth.

Beyond medicine and design, I advocate for feminism, gender equality, veganism, and environmental conservation.
