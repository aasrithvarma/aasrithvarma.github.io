About

Short & meaningful first impression

I’m a clinician and researcher with a deep interest in pediatric cardiac surgery, accessible healthcare and sustainable living. I founded Savière — an initiative focused on vegan, upcycled, and affordable slow-design — and I authored The Power of 77. I actively advocate for feminism, gender-equality, veganism and environmental conservation.
