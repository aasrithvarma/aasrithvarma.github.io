About Me

I am a medical doctor from Hyderabad, India, with aspirations of becoming a pediatric cardiac surgeon. Alongside my medical career, I am deeply passionate about sustainable living, writing, and design. My debut book, The Power of 77, reflects my journey in thought, discipline, and self-discovery.

Aspirations

  • Advance pediatric cardiac surgery and improve access to healthcare in India.
  • Continue writing and contributing to literature that inspires growth and reflection.
  • Promote sustainable, vegan, and eco-conscious lifestyles through thoughtful design.
  • Travel, learn languages, and explore diverse cultures to broaden global perspectives.

My Book

The Power of 77 is a personal exploration of discipline, thought, and the patterns that shape our lives. It encourages readers to reflect and find meaning in everyday actions.
