A Brief Story

Born in Visakhapatnam and raised in Hyderabad, my journey in medicine has taken me across continents, cultures, and healthcare systems. I began my medical education in Ukraine before relocating during the conflict and completing my medical degree in Armenia. Returning to India, I gained exposure to tertiary healthcare systems at KIMS Hospitals, Hyderabad, and later completed my compulsory rotatory internship at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Medical Sciences, Adilabad. These experiences reinforced values that continue to guide me today: resilience, adaptability, compassion, and an enduring commitment to learning.
