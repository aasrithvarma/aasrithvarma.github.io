# Research Initiatives

My research methodology bridges the gap between traditional surgical sciences and modern computational modeling to improve outcomes in congenital heart disease.

## Core Domains

### 1. Pediatric Cardiac Surgery
Investigating optimal cannulation strategies, myocardial protection in neonates, and long-term neurodevelopmental outcomes following complex congenital reconstructions.

### 2. Medical Artificial Intelligence
The development of interpretable machine learning models for early detection of postoperative low cardiac output syndrome (LCOS) in the pediatric intensive care unit.

### 3. Exercise Physiology
Studying the cardiovascular adaptations of endurance training and applying these principles to pediatric cardiac rehabilitation.
